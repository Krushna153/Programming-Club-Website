Programming club

Programming Club (or PClub) is a community of students highly enthusiastic about coding, ethical hacking, Web Designing and all other aspects of programming.

We organize activities related to Development (Web Development, Open Source, Game/App Development), programming contests, hackathons, Capture The Flag contests, et all. Lecture series are regularly taken on algorithms, web hijacking, development, among other things.

During summers, we offer a large number of projects to the every year students through our online and offline workshops/seminars. The 2020 Summer Website Making Ccompetion saw close to 200 participants.

Feel free to contact any of our members any time in case of any queries!
